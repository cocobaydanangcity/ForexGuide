## 2. Legal Framework and Regulation

Forex trading in Kenya is **regulated by the Capital Markets Authority (CMA)**. Only brokers with a valid CMA license are allowed to operate legally in the country. This ensures protection against fraud and gives traders peace of mind. ✅
